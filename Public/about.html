Our Story

Built for Khulna.
Built for the Future.

KuetTrack was born inside the labs of Khulna University of Engineering & Technology — a student-led IoT project turned real urban mobility platform.

Our Mission

Smarter Cities Start with Smarter Transport

We believe urban mobility should be clean, affordable, and accessible to everyone. KuetTrack is our answer — a fully integrated IoT bike-sharing system that uses RFID, GPS, and cloud technology to make cycling in Khulna effortless.

What started as a final-year engineering project at KUET has grown into a fully operational platform, serving students, daily commuters, and city residents across Khulna.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Everything we build is guided by these principles.

🌱
Sustainability First

Every bike trip replaces a CNG or rickshaw ride. We track and publish our collective CO₂ savings monthly — because accountability matters.

🔬
Engineering Excellence

We're engineers at heart. We obsess over uptime, latency, and reliability. 99.8% uptime isn't an accident — it's a design choice.

🤝
Accessible to All

Our student plan exists because we believe affordable transport is a right, not a privilege. KUET students ride at half price.

Privacy & Security

User data is encrypted end-to-end. We never sell your information. RFID credentials are stored as secure hashes, never in plaintext.

We document our IoT protocols and publish research papers. Khulna's smart city future is best built on open, shared knowledge.

Local First

Built in Khulna, for Khulna. We understand the roads, the weather, the commutes. That's why KuetTrack works where generic solutions fail.

Our Journey

From Lab to City Streets

💡
2022
The Idea

A group of KUET CSE & EEE students get frustrated with the lack of affordable last-mile transport on campus. The concept for an RFID-based campus bike system is born during a hackathon.

🔧
2023 Q1
Hardware Prototype

First working prototype: an Arduino-based RFID reader wired to a solenoid lock on a bicycle. 5 test bikes deployed on the KUET campus.

🖥️
2023 Q3
Software Platform Launched

Full backend API, user dashboard, and admin panel built. GPS tracking integrated. 50 users onboarded in the first week on campus.

🚀
2024 Q2
City Expansion

Partnered with Khulna City Corporation. Expanded to 18 docking stations across the city, 188 bikes, and opened to the general public.

🌟
2025
12,000 Users & Growing

KuetTrack crosses 12,000 registered users, 99.8% uptime, and wins the Bangladesh ICT Innovation Award for Urban Mobility solutions.

🔮
2026 →
Electric Bikes & Expansion

E-bike fleet integration underway. Targeting 500 bikes, 50 stations, and expansion into Jessore and Barisal by end of 2026.
