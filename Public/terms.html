01

Eligibility

To use KuetTrack, you must be at least 15 years old and physically capable of safely operating a bicycle. By creating an account, you confirm that you meet these requirements.

University students must verify their institution email during registration to qualify for the Student Plan discount.

02

Account & RFID Card

Each user may hold only one active KuetTrack account. Your RFID card is personal and non-transferable — sharing it with another person is a violation of these terms and will result in account suspension.

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials and RFID card secure. Report a lost or stolen card immediately to support@kuettrack.com to prevent unauthorized rides billed to your account.

💳 Replacement RFID cards cost ৳30 and are issued within 2 working days of a verified request.

03

Acceptable Use

You agree to use KuetTrack bikes only for lawful personal transport. The following are prohibited:

  • Riding under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Carrying more than one person on a single bike
  • Using the bike for commercial delivery, hire, or subletting
  • Tampering with or attempting to disable the GPS unit, RFID reader, or lock mechanism
  • Taking a bike outside of Khulna city limits without prior written approval
  • Leaving a bike in a private compound, inside a building, or in a location that obstructs public access
  • Racing, performing stunts, or riding on footpaths where prohibited
04

Billing & Payments

KuetTrack operates on a prepaid wallet system. You must maintain sufficient balance before unlocking a bike. Rides will not start if your wallet balance is below ৳10.

Fare is calculated based on ride duration at the rate in effect at the time of your ride. The final fare is deducted automatically when you tap your RFID card to end the session. All transactions are final unless there is a verified system error.

🔄 Wallet top-ups are non-refundable but your balance does not expire as long as your account is active.

Rides & Safety

You are responsible for your own safety during every ride. Before riding, inspect the bike briefly for visible damage — flat tyres, broken brakes, or damaged lights. If you find a defect, do not ride the bike. End the session and report it via the app or by calling +880 1234 567890.

You agree to obey all Bangladeshi traffic laws while riding. Wearing a helmet is strongly recommended. KuetTrack does not provide helmets.

⚠️ Riding without proper care or in violation of traffic laws is your sole responsibility. KuetTrack is not liable for injuries resulting from user negligence.

07

Bike Damage

If you damage a bike during your rental period (beyond normal wear and tear), you may be liable for repair costs. We assess damage based on the difference between the bike's condition at check-out (verified by GPS and sensor data) and check-in.

We will notify you by email within 48 hours of a damage assessment and provide evidence before charging. You have 5 days to dispute the assessment.

08

Theft & Loss

If a bike is stolen while in your active session, you must report it to the police and share the police report number with us within 24 hours at ops@kuettrack.com. Failure to report may result in liability for the full replacement cost.

KuetTrack GPS tracking assists police in bike recovery. Do not attempt to recover a stolen bike yourself.
