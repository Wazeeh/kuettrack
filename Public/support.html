FAQ
Common Questions
My RFID card isn't unlocking the bike ›
First check your wallet balance is above ৳10 in your dashboard. Then tap the card flat against the reader and hold for 2 full seconds. If it still fails, the card may need re-registration — email ops@kuettrack.com with your card number and bike ID.
I lost my RFID card. What should I do immediately? ›
Email support@kuettrack.com immediately with your registered email to deactivate the old card. This prevents anyone else from using it. Replacement cards cost ৳30 and are issued within 2 working days.
How do I top up my wallet? ›
Log into your dashboard and click "Add Balance". We accept bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and debit/credit cards. The minimum top-up is ৳50. Balance reflects instantly after payment confirmation.
I was charged but the bike didn't unlock. How do I get a refund? ›
This happens occasionally during brief connectivity drops. Email support@kuettrack.com with the approximate time of your ride attempt. Our team verifies via server logs and refunds are processed back to your wallet within 24 hours.
How is my fare calculated? ›
Rides on the Basic plan cost ৳2/min after a free 10-minute grace period. Monthly Pass holders get unlimited 60-minute rides; excess time is ৳3/min. Fare is calculated automatically and deducted when you tap your card to end the session.
How do I find the nearest available bike? ›
Log into your dashboard and use the live map. Green markers show available bikes; grey markers are in use. You can also filter by station if you have a preferred pick-up point.
What happens if I can't find a docking station to return the bike? ›
Call our emergency line immediately (+880 1234 567890). Do not leave the bike unattended. We can locate the nearest available station on your behalf. Bikes left outside stations incur a ৳200 fine plus ৳5/hour until retrieved.
I found a damaged bike. Should I ride it? ›
No. If you notice flat tyres, broken brakes, or damaged lights — do not ride. End or cancel the session in the app and report the bike ID and location to ops@kuettrack.com. You will not be charged for a session you cancelled due to a defect.
How do I change my password? ›
Go to your dashboard → Account Settings → Change Password. You'll need to enter your current password. If you've forgotten it, click "Forgot Password" on the login page to receive a reset email.
How do I cancel my monthly subscription? ›
Go to Account Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. There are no cancellation fees. Your wallet balance remains available for pay-per-ride use.
How do I delete my account? ›
Account deletion is available in Account Settings → Delete Account. Personal data is purged within 30 days. Note: wallet balance is non-refundable after voluntary deletion. Payment records are retained for 5 years as required by Bangladesh financial regulations.
