Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Visit the full support centre →

My RFID card isn't unlocking the bike. What do I do?

First, check that your wallet balance is above ৳10. If it is, try tapping more firmly and holding for 2 seconds. If it still fails, email ops@kuettrack.com with your card number and bike ID.

I was charged but the bike didn't unlock. How do I get a refund?

This is rare but happens during connectivity issues. Email support@kuettrack.com with your ride attempt time. Refunds are processed within 24 hours.

How do I top up my wallet?

Log into your dashboard and click "Add Balance". We accept bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and debit cards. The balance reflects instantly after payment confirmation.

I found a damaged or abandoned bike. What should I do?

Thank you for reporting! Please note the bike's ID number (printed on the frame) and its location, then email ops@kuettrack.com. Our team will respond within 2 hours.

How do I cancel or change my monthly plan?

You can cancel your plan anytime from your Account Settings. Your access continues until the end of the billing period. No cancellation fees, ever.

Can I get a replacement RFID card?

Yes. Visit any KuetTrack station during staffed hours, or mail us your student ID and account email. Replacement cards cost ৳30 and are issued within 2 working days.
