01

Who We Are

KuetTrack ("we", "our", "us") is an IoT-based smart bike rental and GPS tracking platform operated by the KuetTrack Project Team, KUET Campus, Khulna-9203, Bangladesh.

We are the data controller for all personal information collected through our website, mobile app, RFID system, and GPS infrastructure. For data-related enquiries, contact us at privacy@kuettrack.com.

03

How We Use Your Data

  • To verify your identity and allow RFID-based bike unlocking
  • To calculate and deduct ride fares from your wallet
  • To display your ride history and usage analytics in the app
  • To send transactional notifications (ride start, ride end, low balance, payment confirmation)
  • To detect unauthorized bike movement and trigger anti-theft alerts
  • To improve system reliability, resolve disputes, and debug issues
  • To generate anonymised fleet statistics (no personal data in these reports)

📌 We do not use your data for advertising, profiling for third parties, or any commercial purpose beyond operating the KuetTrack service.

04

GPS & Location Data

GPS tracking is performed on the bike hardware only, never on your personal phone. The GPS module transmits the bike's coordinates to our server during an active rental session.

Location data is used for: live tracking during your ride, route history in your ride summary, station return verification, and anti-theft alerts. GPS transmission stops the moment you end your session by tapping your RFID card to return the bike.

Historical GPS data is stored for 12 months for dispute resolution. After 12 months, precise route data is deleted and only anonymised aggregate statistics (e.g. "Station A to Station B, 4.2km") are retained.

05

RFID Data

Your RFID card's UID is stored as a one-way SHA-256 hash in our database. This means we cannot reverse the hash back to the original UID — it serves only as a lookup key.

We do not share RFID data with any third party. If you lose your card, the old hash is deactivated and a new one is registered for your replacement card.

06

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Payment Processors: Transaction IDs are shared with bKash/Nagad/Rocket to confirm top-up payments. No other personal data is shared.
  • Khulna City Corporation: Anonymised, aggregated fleet usage statistics (no personal data) are shared for city planning purposes.
  • Law Enforcement: If required by valid Bangladeshi legal order, we will provide the minimum data necessary to comply.
  • Service Providers: Our cloud hosting provider (secured under NDA) processes data solely to run our servers.
07

Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted via TLS 1.3
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a unique salt per user
  • RFID UIDs are stored as SHA-256 hashes — never in plaintext
  • Database access is restricted to authorised personnel only, enforced by role-based access control
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing are conducted by KUET CSE faculty
  • Automatic session expiry after 30 days of inactivity

⚠️ If you believe your account has been compromised, email security@kuettrack.com immediately and we will disable your account within 1 hour.

08

Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active + 2 years after deletion for legal compliance
  • Precise GPS route data: 12 months from ride date
  • Payment records: 5 years (required by Bangladesh financial regulations)
  • RFID logs: 6 months
  • Support emails: 2 years from last correspondence
09

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a full export of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information from your account settings
  • Deletion: Request account deletion — we will purge personal data within 30 days (except legally required records)
  • Portability: Receive your ride history and account data in JSON format
  • Objection: Opt out of non-essential communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@kuettrack.com with your registered email address.

10

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies: one session cookie to keep you logged in, and one preference cookie for UI settings. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. No third-party cookies are present on kuettrack.com.

11

Children's Privacy

KuetTrack is intended for users aged 15 and above (minimum legal cycling age for roads in Bangladesh). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 15. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us and we will delete the account promptly.
