How KuetTrack Works

Four simple steps from registration to riding — powered by IoT, RFID, and real-time GPS tracking.

01
📱
Register & Link Card

Create your account via the app. Register your RFID card to your profile and top up your wallet balance.

02
📍
Find a Bike

Use the live map to find the nearest available bike at any docking station around the city.

03
💳
Tap & Unlock

Tap your RFID card on the bike's reader. The system verifies your identity and unlocks the bike instantly.

04
🛰️
Ride & Return

Your ride is tracked in real time via GPS. Return the bike and tap your card to end the session and auto-deduct fare.

Technology

RFID — Your Smart Key

Your RFID card is your identity at the bike dock. One tap connects you to the entire KuetTrack ecosystem.

  • 1

    Identity Verification

    The reader captures your card's unique UID and checks it against the backend database in milliseconds.

  • 2

    Balance Check

    System confirms your wallet has sufficient balance before allowing the unlock to proceed.

  • 3

    Bike Unlock & Session Start

    The microcontroller activates the solenoid lock, releases the bike, and starts the rental timer.

  • 4

    Return & Auto-Deduction

    Tap again to lock and end the session. Fare is calculated and deducted from your wallet instantly.
